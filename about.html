About SENTINEL

Innovative AI technology for a safer tomorrow

Our Mission

At SENTINEL, our mission is to leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence and facial recognition technology to enhance public safety and support law enforcement agencies. We believe that by providing powerful, reliable tools for criminal identification and real-time alerting, we can help create safer communities and more efficient law enforcement operations.

We're committed to developing technology that respects privacy while effectively addressing security challenges. Our system is designed with strong ethical guidelines and robust security measures to ensure responsible use.

Our Technology

Advanced AI

Our system utilizes state-of-the-art deep learning algorithms specifically trained for accurate facial recognition in various conditions including poor lighting, partial views, and at a distance.

Facial Recognition

SENTINEL's facial recognition technology can match faces against our database with up to 99.7% accuracy, even when faces are partially obscured or at difficult angles.

Real-time Processing

Our system processes video feeds in real-time, analyzing multiple faces simultaneously with minimal latency, allowing for immediate detection and alerting.

Intelligent Alerting

When a match is found, SENTINEL automatically triggers customizable alerts through SMS, voice calls via dwani.ai, and optional email notifications.
